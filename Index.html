Clay Soil Lawn Care Schedule (UK, Sustainable Approach)

Season Goals Recommended Actions
Spring (Mar–May) Wake up the lawn, relieve compaction, boost healthy growth
  • Scarify: Light raking to remove thatch (Mar/Apr)
  • Aerate: Hollow-tine aeration (Apr)
  • Feed: Use Mo Bacter or Westland SafeLawn
  • Top-dress: Optional — mix of loam, sand, compost
Summer (Jun–Aug) Maintain health, manage heat/wear stress
  • Feed: Light feed if needed (e.g. Mo Bacter, NPK 10-5-10)
  • Water: Deep, infrequent watering (early morning)
  • Mow: Keep blades sharp; don't cut below 3–4 cm
Autumn (Sep–Oct) Repair damage, strengthen for winter
  • Scarify: Light raking (early Sep)
  • Aerate: Again for clay (late Sep)
  • Overseed: Use hardwearing mix (fescue/ryegrass)
  • Top-dress: Light layer over seed
  • Feed: Use EverGreen Autumn Lawn Care
Winter (Nov–Feb) Let the lawn rest and recover
  • Keep off grass when frosty or waterlogged
  • Clear fallen leaves
  • No feeding required
